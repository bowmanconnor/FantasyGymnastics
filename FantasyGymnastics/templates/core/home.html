Welcome

To get started, click the login button in the top right, and then the register button on the next page and make an account!

Note: This is your real account, so use your real email address and username as they can’t be changed later (for now at least)

Once you’re logged in, navigate to the My Leagues link in the top left.

Once you’ve created a league, figure out with your league how you want to go through the drafting process. Our suggestion is that you call each other and follow a snake draft (there’ll be a ‘draft’ feature coming soon, but for now you’ll need to draft gymnasts yourself).

The manager of the league will also need to set matchups for each week (again, automated matchups are coming soon – for now, we recommend you have an even number of players in the league so that everyone gets to play each week).

Once you have all your players, go ahead and set your lineups for this weekend and play!
