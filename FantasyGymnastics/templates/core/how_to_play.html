How To Play

Welcome!

This tutorial will teach you all you need to know to get started with Fantasy Gymnastics and get you playing right away!

Sign Up for an Account/Login

If you are a New User:

  • From the home page, click on the “Register” button in the top right corner
  • Next, create an account by filling in the required information in the Register form, then click the “Register” button to finish registering your account
    • You may also choose to create an account using your preexisting Facebook or Google account

If you already have an Account:

  • From the home page, click on the “Login” button in the top right corner
  • Next, enter your login username and password in the Login form, and click the “Sign In” button to login
    • You may also choose to login using your preexisting Facebook or Google account

Create/Join a League

Creating a League:

  • From the home page, click the “MyLeagues” tab at the top left of the navigation bar
    • You must be logged into your account to access the MyLeagues page
  • Next, click the “Create New League” button
  • To create a league, you must fill out the required fields:
    • Name – The name of your league
    • Roster size – The total number of gymnasts that can be on a user’s team at one time
    • Lineup size – The total number of gymnasts from a team’s roster allowed to compete during a matchup at the same time
    • Event lineup size – How many gymnasts will be put up to compete on each event
    • Event count size – How many gymnasts’ scores will count on each event towards your final team score
  • Finally, click the “Submit” button to create your league

Joining a League:

  • From the home page, click the “MyLeagues” tab at the top left of the navigation bar
    • You must be logged into your account to access the MyLeagues page
  • Next, click on the “Join Existing League” button to see all the current leagues
  • From the league search page, you can search for a league or click on a league from the list
  • After selecting a league, you may request to join that league by clicking the “Request to join league” button
  • Once you have requested to join a league, the league owner must approve your request to allow you to join by clicking the request at the bottom of the league page, below the “Requested to Join” section
    • To accept a request, simply click the request
    • To decline a request, click the “X” on the far-right side of the request
  • After accepting the request, both users’ teams should now show up at the top of the league page, below the “Rank” section

The Draft

  • Once all users have joined your league and are ready to begin the draft, the league owner can click the “Start draft” button at the top of the league page
    • Note: You must have an even number of users in your league to begin the draft
  • During the draft, all users will take turns drafting gymnasts to their teams
    • In the top left of the page, you can see whose turn it is to draft
  • To draft a gymnast, simply click the “Add” button next to the gymnast’s name
    • You may search for gymnasts in the search bar by their name, team name, or scores
    • The table shows all the gymnast’s averages on the events that they have competed on throughout the season
    • You may filter the table based on any of the columns
  • Once every user has filled their roster with gymnasts, the draft is completed!

Add/Drop from Roster

Adding to your roster:

  • From your league page, click on your team in the “Rank” section
  • Then, to pick up a new gymnast from the available gymnasts, click plus icon below your roster
    • You must have an empty spot on your roster to pick up a new gymnast, otherwise, you can only view the available gymnasts
  • Finally, click the “Add” button next to the gymnast you want to pick up to successfully add the gymnast to your roster

Dropping from your roster:

  • From your league page, click on your team in the “Rank” section
  • Then, to drop a gymnast from your roster, simply click on the “X” button next to the gymnast you wish to drop

Set Lineups

  • From your league page, click on your team in the “Rank” section
  • Then, from your team page, set your lineups by selecting the event you wish to set the lineup for in the “Lineups” section
  • To add a gymnast from your roster to the selected event lineup, click on the plus icon below the lineup and click on the name of the gymnast you wish to add
    • To remove a gymnast from an event lineup, simply click on the event and click on the “X” next to the gymnast you wish to remove

The Rules of the Game

  • Once you have joined a league and drafted your gymnasts, you are ready to begin playing the game!
  • Each week you will be automatically matched up against another user in your league and compete in a head-to-head matchup
  • Every week you need to set your lineups for each event based on which gymnasts from your roster you think will score the highest during their meets
  • When setting your event lineups, you will put up multiple gymnasts that will accrue scores for your team, increasing your overall team score
    • The number of gymnasts to put up on each lineup is decided by the event lineup size in the league’s settings
  • Only the top scores of your gymnasts on each event lineup will be counted towards your overall team score
    • The number of counting scores per event is decided by the event count size in the league’s settings
  • Once the week is over and all scores have been inputted for the week, the user with the highest team score will win their head-to-head matchup
    • All the gymnasts' scores and final team scores will be automatically updated after every meet
  • Throughout the season, as you win and lose your head-to-head matchups against other users in your league, your season win-to-loss record will be updated accordingly
  • The final rankings will be determined by the teams’ win-to-loss records at the end of the season, and whoever has the best record, wins their league!

You are now ready to start playing Fantasy Gymnastics!
